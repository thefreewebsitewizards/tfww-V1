Small Business Owner Questions

We partner with hosting companies who pay us commissions for bringing them customers. This allows us to offer completely free website development while still running a sustainable business. Your small business gets a professional website at no cost, and our hosting partners get new customers.

There is absolutely no catch and no hidden fees. The website development is completely free. The only ongoing cost is hosting, which you pay directly to our hosting partner (typically $10-20/month). We're transparent about this from the start - perfect for small business budgets.

Most small business websites are completed within 7-14 business days. We understand small businesses need to get online quickly. The timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements and how quickly you provide content and feedback during the development process.

Yes, you own your website completely. We build it for you, but you have full ownership and control. You can make changes, move it to another host, or do whatever you want with it. This is important for small business independence.

We work with all types of local businesses including restaurants, service companies, retail stores, professional services, healthcare practices, and more. If you're a legitimate small business that needs an online presence, we can help.

We provide ongoing support designed for small business owners. Minor changes are often included, and we offer very reasonable rates for larger modifications or additional features. We understand small businesses evolve and grow.
